Privacy Policy

This app is a personal football analytics tool. Here is how your data is handled:

  • API key: Your football-data.org API key is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. It is never sent to any server other than football-data.org.
  • Google Sign-in: Used only to sync your imported historical season data across your devices via Firebase Firestore. Your Google account details (name, email, photo) are displayed in the app header only.
  • Historical data: If you are signed in, imported CSV match data is stored in your private Firebase Firestore account under your user ID. Only you can access it.
  • No tracking: This app contains no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking of any kind.
  • No data sharing: Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.
